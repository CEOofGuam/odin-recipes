Greek Pasta Salad

Description

A very easy, refreshing, and cost effective meal. Tastes better the next day, and goes well with a protein choice like Italian sausage. This is one of the first recipes Kait and I made together.

Ingredients

  • Rotini Pasta
  • English Cucumber
  • Diced Bell Pepper
  • Sliced Red Onion
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Feta Cheese
  • Kalamata Olives

Greek Dressing

  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Dried Oregano
  • Lemon Zest and Lemon Juice
  • Salt and Freshly Ground Black Pepper
